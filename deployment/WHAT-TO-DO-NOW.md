## What This System Does

This is a **monitoring dashboard** for your multi-agent workflow system. It:

1. **Monitors agents** - Shows what your agents are doing
2. **Tracks repositories** - Lists repositories being analyzed
3. **Shows ideas** - Displays synthesized ideas from analysis
4. **Reports incidents** - Shows errors and warnings
